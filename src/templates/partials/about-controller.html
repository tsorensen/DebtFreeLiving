About Us

Einar Schow was one of the co-founders of Debt Free Living in 1996, and served as the President and CEO. He was instrumental in creating the original Debt Elimination Plan, which assisted American Families in the quest to become completely debt free, including their mortgage. Einar has a long history of helping families design and successfully implement their debt elimination plans by giving them access to effective tools and educating them on the how to eliminate debt as quickly and effectively as possible.

Over the years, Einar has continued to be a student of all of the processes and products available for helping people become debt free. The underlying principles of debt elimination are simple to understand, but require a commitment to a consistent application of a plan. This web-site is dedicated to helping people understand how to eliminate their debt.
